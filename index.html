💡Key Learnings

What I'd do again.
What I'd change.

1
🎯 Enterprise users have zero tolerance for ambiguity

Every label and empty state needed to be explicit. Ops managers scan under pressure — clarity became the first design principle.

2
⚡ Animation adds trust, not just delight

The login animation communicated system state, not decoration. In enterprise tools, intentional motion builds confidence.

3
🚀 Ship sooner than feels comfortable

My best decisions came from watching real users on the floor. No prototype reveals what daily operational use does.

4
📊 Data and design must speak the same language

Connecting Power BI data models to the UI decisions taught me that the best dashboards are designed from the data structure up.
